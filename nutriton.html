The Seven Food Groups

  • Carbohydrates
  • Fats
  • Fiber
  • Minerals
  • Protein
  • Vitamins
  • Water

Why we need Seven

These are the basic catagories of food necessary in a healthy diet. Carbohydrates provide energy and help your body get quick easy to break down nutriton for exercise and other activites. Fats provide energy that is stored and requires more work to break down. Fiber is used to help our digestive system function properly.

Minerals are needed to help us in many ways the body needs them for muscles movement bone strength and other necessary functions. Protein helps us build and repair tissue in the body. Vitamins are necessary all over the body as well because the are needed for almost all bodily functions. Water helps our bodys cells and organs and helps us regulate temperature, breathing, and sweating
